Welcome to Deckstack

You've read the books. You know the advice.
Deckstack is where you practice it.

Communication strategies you rehearse like flashcards — so they're actually there when a real conversation needs them.

Be honest —

How often do you read great advice… and then never get to use it?

That's exactly the gap Deckstack closes: knowing → doing.

What would you like to get better at?

Pick as many as you like.

How do you like to train?

Here's the idea

1

Pick a strategy — like The Broken Record or Negative Inquiry.

2

A card shows you a real situation. Pause. Visualize how you'd respond.

3

Flip the card for a strong example — then make it your own.

A few cards a day, and the strategies start showing up in real conversations.

Or go handsfree

Train with your ears — while walking, doing dishes, or commuting.

1

The situation on the card is read aloud to you.

2

You get thinking time to form your own response in your head.

3

Then a strong suggested response is read aloud — compare it with yours.

Everything is adjustable in the handsfree settings — like how much thinking time you get, the voice, and the pace.

About Deckstack

Deckstack is a communication training app. You've read the books — Deckstack is where you practice: rehearse communication strategies like flashcards until they show up in real conversations.

Version

Privacy policy

Your data stays on your device. Deckstack has no accounts and no server. Everything the app knows — your progress, favorites, settings, and unlocked content — is stored locally on this device and never sent anywhere.

No tracking. Deckstack contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Speech. Handsfree audio uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. No audio or text is sent to external services.

Third-party resources. The app loads its icon font from a content delivery network (jsDelivr). Like any web request, that transfer includes your IP address; no other information is shared.

Backups. The Export function in Settings creates a copy of your data that only you hold. Deckstack never uploads it.

Deleting your data. Since everything is local, removing the app (or clearing its website data) permanently deletes all your Deckstack data.

Questions about privacy? Contact the developer.
